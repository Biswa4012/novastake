What chains do you support?
EVM (Ethereum, Polygon, Base, Optimism, Arbitrum, and more) plus select non‑EVM networks. New chains are added regularly.
How does the free tier work?
The Builder plan includes generous monthly request limits across mainnets and testnets. No credit card required to start.
Can I migrate from another provider?
Yes. We provide migration guides, one‑click RPC switching, and optional white‑glove assistance for complex setups.
Is NOVASTAKE suitable for Hedgefunds?
Absolutely—SLAs, SSO/SAML, audit logs, VPC peering, and custom compliance support are available on Enterprise plans.
